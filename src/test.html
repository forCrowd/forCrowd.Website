ABOUT

forCrowd is a freelance consultancy specializing in software development, digital transformation, and IT recruitment. We seek to meet the tailored needs of each business, delivering high-quality solutions.

We also have a unique passion for open technologies, such as open source software, hardware, and AI. We actively research and develop strategies to utilize the potential of open technologies at a large scale to drive productivity, foster tech sovereignty, and maximize innovation.

Serkan Holat, the founder and principal consultant, is a seasoned professional with over two decades of hands-on experience in the software industry. He has been dedicated to crafting enterprise solutions since 2001, and his expertise spans diverse sectors, including finance, chemical manufacturing, media, and information technologies.
